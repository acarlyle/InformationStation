Dashboards

EagleJobs provides you with a dashboard, where you can access all the features included for each type of user. Your user profile will be displayed on your dashboard, as well as the ability to search at the top. Students and employers will have different newsfeeds containing important information pertaining to each individual user. Bookmarks will be displayed, and you can even edit your profile directly on the dashboard!

On EagleJobs, students and employers have different features for their respective profiles. Students are able to display information an employer may want to know, while Employer profiles allow companies to share their mission statement, as well as other relevant information about the company.
