To continue in education and be formally admitted into the Graduate Degree Program, students must do the following:

  1. meet the criteria listed for program continuation,
  2. successfully complete required undergraduate course work with an overall GPA of 2.5 or better and a GPA of 3.0 or better in required education courses,
  3. receive a bachelor's degree from UMW,
  4. achieve passing scores on Praxis II, the Virginia Communication and Literacy Assessment (VCLA), and for those students in the Elementary program and Special Education: General Curriculum program, the Reading for Virginia Educators (RVE) assessment. (Please note, the College of Education must have copies of all required test scores.)

If you need to take an exam, you will be asked to provide the exam name and the scheduled test date.
